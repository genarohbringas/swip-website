Who We Are

More Than a Sport.
A Way of Life.

At Sportika Woodlands Padel, we believe that padel is more than a sport—it's a way of life. Nestled at 8626 West Ln, Magnolia, TX 77354, our club was born from a deep passion for the game and a commitment to building a thriving community of players.

Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or competing at a professional level, our world-class facilities and welcoming atmosphere ensure every visit is unforgettable.
